About me

Hello everyone, my name is Josias.

I am 25 years old former City Tech student. I started college majoring in Computer Engineer and after a year and half I decided to switch majors. I was having a difficult time deciding on a major, I only thought about Computer Engineering but I had no idea what exactly I want it to do. I decided on taking on Accounting in which I got my AAS despite not being as interested in the topic.

What made you want to be a Programmer?

During the lockdown I had time to think about what I want it to do, I think that's when I realized that I needed a change in my life, I just didn't know what it was. I heard about programming from my wife's long time friend who is a Front-end Developer and the way he talked about it I instantly became interestedin in it and decided to give it a try.

I started teaching myself how to code watching Youtube tutorials and taking Udemy courses. I was struggling a lot trying and whoever is a self-taught programmer knows is not easy. I learned Html, Css and a bit of JavaScript but I was still not able to have a strong grasp in the subject and I sometimes felt like an imposter, just following tutorials in order to build a project.

Why Coding Temple?

I needed help

If you're like me (self-taught) you probably have gone through the same issue, failed to learn on your own. All you hear on the internet is that bootcamps are expensive, you shouldn't go to one, you can learn all that on your own. In reality all that is true but I needed help in order to get where I wanted. I feel confident with Html and Css but I have no experience at all with another language. Coding Temple has giving me confidence I never thought I had in order to do things like understanding data structure and algorithms. I decided to join this program mostly because I want a change in my career and I know that with my passion and love for coding I will get there.
